Guided Hikes

Casita Sedona offers a free guided hike each week for guests. Explore the Seven Sacred Pools along the Soldiers Pass Trail.

Located in the Heart of Sedona

At Casita Sedona Bed & Breakfast you’ll be close to art galleries, shops, restaurants, hiking trails, and tours. Ride the free troley to shops and galleries.

Luxurious Rooms

Stay in a well-appointed room at Casita Sedona with your own fireplace, king-size bed, balcony overlooking the red rocks, private bath with shower, and whirlpool tub for two. Choose from the Canyonk, Javelina, Manzanita, Thunder Mountan, and Schnebly rooms — eacy is unique an sure to be a high point of your vactaion experience.

Hearty Breakfast

Each day begins with a hearty breakfast that includes your choice of belgian waffles, pumpkin french toast, or quiche. Fresh fruit, yogurt, juice, and coffee are always available.

About Your Hosts

Your hosts, Melanie and Greg Tortuga, are long-time Sedona residents who love to share their knowledge of the Sedona area wtih their guests. Melanie is an author and artist. Greg has years of experience leading jeep tours and guiding hikes in and around Sedona.
